Hey, I'm Javier

Learn a bit about me!

I'm a product designer with a background in User Experience and Marketing

I've always been interested in how to improve products, user interfaces, and designs in general
in order to make them not only more inclusive, but taking those inclusivity changes and making them
beneficial to every single user. I've written multiple papers while obtaining my graduate degree
on various subjects revolving around inclusivity and have have made it one of my primary goals
to challenge status quos which often have ignored disadvantaged groups.

More About Me!


  • I did marketing for a UC Santa Cruz video game community and increased event sign-ups and attendance by over 100%

  • I love to write. Some of my favorite topics I've written about have been non-verbal communication in virtual reality, and online communities acting as supportive pseudo-cultures for LGBTQ individuals

  • I volunteered to create the initial Love Smiles website, which spreads awareness about pediatric cancer and sends gift packages to children in hospitals.
