Additional Design Work

Click on any of my projects below to learn more, or feel free to contact me for more information.

Netflix Theatre App Concept

This course project asked the question, "What if Netflix wanted to enter the movie theatre market?" Goals included promotion plans, detailed information and media for new and upcoming releases, and continued use plan for users.

Harrowing: A Legends of Runeterra Event Concept

Taking inspiration from Riot Games' Harrowing League of Legends event, I decided to conceptualize and design this work to act as a hypothetical cross-game tie-in for their Legends of Runeterra game.

Team Fight Tactics Mobile Team Planner Redesign

A quick design project focused on redesigning the planning page for the mobile version of Riot's TeamFight Tactics game The changes made were aimed to create a more robust, detailed plan for once the player is in the game.

ExplorAR

ExplorAR was conceptualized as a AR-focused urban exploration mobile application during my time at DePaul, which gave users curated destinations where they could then use AR-functionality to explore the site interactively.

Anilist Mobile Application

This prototype was designed for a graduate school project and presented various functions and pages of a potential mobile version of Anilist, a web-first anime tracker website.

TCGPlayer AI Landing Page

This design project was my first attempt to use AI-generated images as a design inspiration tool. Much like the classic "Crazy-Eights" UX exercise, AI can be used to rapidly create multiple designs which can then be fleshed out further.

Food Delivery Manager Dashboard

A design for a potential web-based dashboard managing food deliveries. The goal was lessening the need for the manager to switch between pages in order to streamline their responsibilities and information intake.

Nintendo Online Tablet Application Redesign

This redesigned Nintendo Online tablet application was a quick project aimed at tailoring the experience for tablet's instead of the then mirroring of their mobile phone app, using the larger screen space to lessen the travel between multiple different pages.
