This course project asked the question, "What if Netflix wanted to enter the movie theatre market?" Goals included promotion plans, detailed information and media for new and upcoming releases, and continued use plan for users.

ExplorAR

ExplorAR was conceptualized as an AR-focused urban exploration mobile application during my time at DePaul, which gave users curated destinations where they could then use AR functionality to explore the site interactively.
